About

Code Club is a student run club for those with an interest in computer science or computing in general. It was made to develop a coding community around all the college. Though we are a university club, we welcome anyone interested in computer science and/or socialising to join!

Members will have access to:

  • Computer science discussions
  • Brainstorming sessions - where we will pick some problems and work together on it as a group. Discuss the approach we are going to take and try implementations in various programming languages.
  • Open Discussion where we will invite a member, faculty adviser or guest to lead a discussion on a topic of their choice related to computing, this could include: Web Development, Android Development, Machine Learning, Manual vs. Automated Testing or any other topic.
  • Fun and gaming events
  • Hold regular programming contests for practise
  • And many more...
